Our Story

Established in October 2018, Universal Vet is committed to delivering professional pet care. Our fully equipped facility boasts modern technology and a qualified team offering OPD treatments, surgeries, grooming, dental care, pet boarding, and more. Our Pet Boarding & Hospitalization service ensures worry-free vacations for pet owners. Additionally, we conduct mobile spay/neuter programs nationwide to combat rabies and address the issue of homeless puppies, collaborating with animal welfare organizations in Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing the best possible care and service. We realize that your pets are a part of your family and we are dedicated to ensuring your pets are treated with tender loving care.
